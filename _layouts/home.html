Heyo! I'm Da​Cube​King.

That's my pen name, irl people know me as Varun.

I'm a 20-year-old college student from California who loves programming and tinkering with computers. I learn by doing and am not afraid to tackle projects that push me to develop new skills—it's what led to this website and many of my other projects.

Naturally, I'm majoring in Computer Science with a Math minor, but outside of coding, I also love exploring video production and photography, always seeking ways to blend my creative side with tech.

In high school, I was the programming lead for the FRC team 3476 - Code Orange, where I earned several programming awards. I developed some of the top-performing localization code (powering autonomous routines) that helped lead us to a division win during my senior year.

I also spent a good amount of time making YouTube videos, mainly focused on Minecraft, which became a fun outlet for my more creative and artistic side.

When I'm not working or coding, you might find me watching a rom-com, listening to a lecture on a niche topic, or reading a novel. See what else I've read here.

If I'm not doing the above, you'll likely find me playing video games. I recently finished playing through Alan Wake 2 and am now working my way through Celeste. I also continue to enjoy Minecraft and Valorant.
